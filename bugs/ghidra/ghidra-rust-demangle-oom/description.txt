OOM / UB in Ghidra Bundled libiberty rust_demangle
===================================================

Project:     NationalSecurityAgency/ghidra (bundled GPL libiberty)
Bug class:   null-deref / oom (UBSan/ASan dependent on input)
Sanitizer:   UBSan (primary)

Ghidra bundles a copy of GNU libiberty at
`GPL/DemanglerGnu/src/demangler_gnu_v2_41/c/`. The vendored
`rust_demangle()` shares its bugs with the upstream binutils/GCC copy:
`str_buf_append()` does pointer arithmetic on a `str_buf_t` whose
`buf` field can be NULL, and the v0 binder parser has an unbounded
lifetime-count allocation path.

UBSan catches the null-pointer offset before the OOM path on most
malformed inputs.

The harness drives `rust_demangle()` on a NUL-terminated copy of the
fuzz input.
