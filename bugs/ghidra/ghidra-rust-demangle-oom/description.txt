OOM in Ghidra Bundled libiberty rust_demangle
=============================================

Project:     NationalSecurityAgency/ghidra (bundled GPL libiberty)
Bug class:   oom (unbounded output-buffer growth)
Sanitizer:   libFuzzer out-of-memory (built with -fsanitize=fuzzer,address)

Ghidra bundles a copy of GNU libiberty at
`GPL/DemanglerGnu/src/demangler_gnu_v2_41/c/`. The vendored
`rust_demangle()` grows its output buffer with no maximum-size
limit. `str_buf_reserve()` doubles the capacity in a loop until it
covers the requested length and then calls `realloc()` with no cap.

A small (~61-byte) malformed Rust v0 mangled symbol with nested
generic / lifetime parameters drives ~35-million-times output
amplification through

    demangle_type -> demangle_binder -> print_lifetime_from_index
    -> str_buf_append -> str_buf_reserve -> realloc

The single realloc request reaches 2 GB, libFuzzer's malloc hook
trips, and stderr ends with
`SUMMARY: libFuzzer: out-of-memory`.

The harness drives `rust_demangle()` on a NUL-terminated copy of the
fuzz input.
